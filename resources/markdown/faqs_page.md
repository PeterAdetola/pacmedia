# Got Questions?|We Have Answers.

---
WORKING WITH US
---
How do I get started with Pacmedia?
Everything begins with a Tactical Briefing — a consultation where we learn about your business, goals, and where you're headed. You can initiate this directly on the website. From there, if there's strategic alignment and your brief qualifies, we'll move forward with onboarding. We don't take on every project — we take on the right ones.

---
What makes you selective? How do you vet partners?
We limit our active projects intentionally so every client gets the full weight of our attention — not a template and a handoff. We look for founders and businesses that are serious about their brand, understand the value of well-engineered digital systems, and have a clear direction they're building toward. If we're not the right fit for your brief, we'll tell you honestly and point you in the right direction.

---
Do you work with clients outside Nigeria?
Yes — and increasingly so. Our entire process is built to work remotely: discovery, approvals, feedback, and delivery all happen through structured digital touchpoints. Time zone differences are manageable; we coordinate around your schedule. All project communication and invoicing is conducted in English, and payments are handled in USD.

---
How long does a typical project take?
Timelines depend on the scope. As a general guide: Brand Identity projects run 2–4 weeks from discovery to final delivery. Website design and development projects typically run 4–8 weeks depending on complexity. Web applications (inventory systems, CRMs, custom platforms) are scoped individually — usually 6–16 weeks. All timelines are confirmed during the briefing stage and tracked transparently throughout the project.

THE BRAND IDENTITY PROCESS
---
What happens after the initial consultation?
Once your project is confirmed, you'll receive access to a private client portal where you'll complete a Brand Discovery form. This covers your business name, vision, mission, target audience, competitors, and a set of brand direction sliders. This gives us everything we need to move into concept development without guessing.

---
How many logo concepts will I receive?
We present 2 to 3 distinct logo directions — each grounded in the brand discovery data and developed through hand sketching before moving to digital execution. We don't generate random variations; every concept has a strategic rationale. You'll review them with that context in mind, not just visual preference.

---
Do you create a moodboard before designing?
Yes. Before any logo concepts are developed, we build a moodboard that establishes the visual direction — color language, typographic tone, imagery style, and overall feel. This is presented for your approval before design begins, so we're aligned on direction before we invest time in execution.

---
How many revision rounds are included?
Each project includes a defined number of revision rounds, confirmed at the briefing stage. Revisions apply to refinements within a chosen direction — not switching to an entirely different concept. Revision scope beyond the agreed rounds is billed separately and discussed transparently before proceeding.

---
What do I receive at the end of a brand identity project?
Final deliverables include your logo in all required file formats (SVG, PNG, PDF), across all necessary variations (primary, alternate, icon/favicon, light and dark versions), a brand style guide covering colors, typography, spacing, and usage rules. Everything is packaged so you — or any future designer or developer — can use your brand consistently without guesswork.

WEB DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT
---
What technology do you build web applications with?
Our core stack for web applications is Laravel (PHP) on the backend — a robust, production-grade framework used by serious engineering teams globally. For systems like inventory management, CRMs, client portals, and custom dashboards, Laravel gives us the architectural control to build something that scales with your business, not against it.

---
Will I be able to update my website content myself after launch?
Yes — where applicable. Websites that require frequent content updates are built with a content management interface so you can make changes without touching code. The scope of what's editable is defined during the project brief. We also offer post-launch support arrangements for clients who prefer to hand off all maintenance.

---
Do you handle hosting and domain setup?
We can advise on and set up hosting environments, configure domains, and deploy your project to a live server. Hosting costs are separate from our project fees and depend on your chosen provider and infrastructure requirements. We'll recommend the right setup for your scale and budget during the briefing.

PROJECT TRACKING & COMMUNICATION
---
How will I know where my project stands at any given time?
Every active client has access to a project dashboard inside the client portal. It shows exactly what has been completed, what's currently in progress, and what comes next — no need to chase us for updates. Major milestones require your sign-off before we proceed, so you're always in the loop.

---
What's your primary communication channel during a project?
Project communication is consolidated inside the client portal to keep everything documented and organized. For international clients, we schedule structured video check-ins at key milestones. We deliberately avoid scattered WhatsApp/email threads — every feedback and decision lives in one place so nothing gets lost.

PRICING & PAYMENTS
---
How much does a project cost?
Project investment is scoped during the Tactical Briefing once we understand the full requirements. What we can say: we price for quality and sustainability, not to win a race to the bottom. If budget is a concern, raise it in the briefing and we'll tell you honestly what's achievable.

---
What are your payment terms?
Projects are typically split into milestone-based payments: a deposit to commence, an interim payment at an agreed midpoint, and a final payment before delivery. The exact structure is confirmed in your project agreement. No work proceeds without a signed agreement and cleared deposit.

---
Do you offer ongoing retainers or post-project support?
Yes. For clients who want continued design support, application maintenance, or iterative development after the initial project, we offer structured retainer arrangements. These are scoped individually based on the volume and nature of work required. Existing clients get priority access to our capacity.
